About Me

I'm a Senior Project Manager based in Lagos, Nigeria, with a proven track record of leading multi-million-dollar programmes across fintech, infrastructure, and enterprise software sectors.

My approach blends rigorous methodology with adaptability — I know when to follow the playbook and when to rewrite it. I bridge the gap between executive vision and ground-level delivery, ensuring every stakeholder is aligned and every deadline is respected.

When I'm not running sprints or steering-committee sessions, I mentor emerging PMs and contribute to the PMI Nigeria community.
